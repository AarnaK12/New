Why does it matter?

Why do we judge other people by the color of their skin? No one really knows, but we still do. And it needs to stop. There was a horrible time when we thought colored people were not equal to white people. We can't let that happen again. There are people leading protests, making their voice heard, and their point clear. Black Lives Matter! Police have acted recklessly and killed black people for unreasonable things. This matter is important, but we need to keep in mind that there is also another global issue happening. The covid-19 pandemic, so while these protests are meant to be peaceful they are also meant to be safe. The protests need to be social-distanced because the numbers are rising as days pass. Also sometimes these protests can get out of hand , so we need to make sure that they are peaceful. We need to stop this, and as Martin Luther King Jr. said, "Darkness cannot drive out darkness; only light can do that. Hate cannot drive out hate; only love can do that."
